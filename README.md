# INTRO-TO-VR-DEVLOPMENT
Hi everyone, I’m Harsh Verma, a student with a strong interest in technology and emerging fields.

I’m excited to share that I’ve started a Virtual Reality (VR) workshop organized by IEEE WIE. This workshop is giving me the opportunity to explore the fundamentals of VR, understand how immersive technologies work, and learn about their real-world applications.

Looking forward to gaining more knowledge and building my understanding in this domain

Through this, I gained hands-on experience with 3D modeling, lighting techniques, and environment design. It marks an important step in my journey into virtual reality and 3D development, and I look forward to building more advanced and creative projects ahead.
